first way:

compile it with executable file alone alien_hunting.exe
in the downloaded executable file execute permissions are denied.
enable the execute permissions by 
chmod u+x alien_hunting.exe
chmod g+x alien_hunting.exe
chmod o+x alien_hunting.exe
then execute it  by ./alien_hunting.exe
